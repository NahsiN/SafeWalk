SafeWalk Find your safest route for any time of day

Project for Insight Data Science Fellowship Fall 2016

Instructions (Video below)

Browse the map on your right. Clicking on the map for the first time will generate a marker and fill in the starting latitude and latitude below. Clicking on the map a second time will populate the ending latitude and longitude fields. Clicking again will repeat this cycle. Once you are happy with the destinations coordinates, select the time of day, crime avoidance level and fear of crime and then click on Route Me!
